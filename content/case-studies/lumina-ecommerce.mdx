# The Challenge
High cart abandonment rates on mobile devices and a brand identity that felt outdated compared to competitors.

# The Solution
Implemented a mobile-first design approach with a sticky 'Add to Bag' bar and one-tap checkout options like Apple Pay.

# Impact & Results
- **25%** Increase in mobile conversion
- **15%** Increase in Average Order Value
- **+40** NPS Score Improvement
