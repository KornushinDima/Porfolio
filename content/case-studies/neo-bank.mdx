# The Challenge
The legacy app suffered from a 65% drop-off rate during the onboarding process and received complaints about complex navigation for simple transfers.

# The Solution
We simplified the sign-up flow from 12 steps to 4, introduced biometric login as a standard, and created a 'Quick Send' widget on the dashboard.

# Impact & Results
- **45%** Increase in onboarding completion
- **-30%** Reduction in support tickets
- **#1** Featured in App Store Finance
