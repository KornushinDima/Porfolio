# The Challenge
Users struggled to interpret the raw health data provided by the device, leading to disuse after the first month.

# The Solution
Created a 'Health Score' algorithm that summarized complex metrics into a single daily number, accompanied by actionable tips.

# Impact & Results
- **60%** Retention rate improvement
- **3x** Increase in daily app opens
- **100%** Apple HealthKit Integration
